Welcome to my App development blog

 

My name is Sebastian Vieira

 

I have been developing mobile apps since 2003 that I started with Nokia's Symbian OS and J2ME. That quickly changed in 2008 that the App Store was introduced and changed everything.

I try to combine my passion for the Swift programming language and being a father. This is how Swiftdaddy was born.

I live in Switzerland where I work as an iOS developer for an awesome company homegate.ch. I have also had the privilege to work for Swisscom at local.ch and a few Swiss Startups.

Finally, I am with my friend and awesome developer Vikram Kriplaney, co-founder of iPhonso GmbH, a Swiss app development company for which we develop fun and interesting apps.
